Sales Pipeline Dashboard

Power BI Dashboard Sharepoint Folder Power Query

Sales team will use this dashboard for their routine meeting. Data source is simple for Small-medium enterprises with Sharepoint folder connected then datasource could be changed to any kind of data warehouse. Please view below the app for users to interact and dive into more insights.

Bi-weekly to review their bi-weekly sales activities, mainly focus on the sales pipeline movement

Bi-weekly Activities

1. New lead: showing new projects coming in the pipeline during the last 2 weeks

2. Movement within pipeline: any projects that have some movement (changes in status, stage)

3 & 4. Deal win / Deal lost: any projects that have been closed in the last 2 weeks

5. No movement within 2 weeks: any projects that have no movement in the last 2 weeks

Sales Pipeline Performance

A monthly pipeline performance showing the health of the pipeline in terms of: win rate, speed of closing deals, and average win values. With these information, we can use to estimate whether the current pipeline is healthy and if target is reachable.

A deep dive into the lost deals to understand the reasons behind the lost deals

Conversion Rate

Conversion rate is built for the collaboration between MKT & Sales team in terms of qualified leads by Sources, Customer Group, Team and Salesman.
